01First things first

Getting started & signing in

Everyone on your team gets their own login. Nothing installs — you just open the website.

  1. Open sarvaexpress-app.web.app on any phone or computer browser. Add it to your home screen for one-tap access.
  2. Enter your email and password and press Sign in.
  3. First time only: use the temporary password your owner shared, then you'll be asked to set your own password. Choose one only you know — from then on that's your password.
The owner account is the first account — it's your master login (your email + the password you set). From it you create everyone else. Forgot your password? Ask another owner/manager to remove and re-invite you, or reach us to reset it.
02Who's who

The four roles

Each person is one of four roles. The role decides what they can see and do. You choose it when you invite them (and can change it any time).

Owner — that's you

The boss. Sees everything, isn't time-tracked, and runs the whole show. You mostly watch and direct while your team does the work.

  • Sees every trip and which supervisor handles it
  • Creates the team & sets each person's permissions
  • Assigns tasks with deadlines, gets alerts when done/overdue
  • Full access to money, reports and exports
Manager

Your right hand. Same full access as the owner — use this for a partner or office head who helps run everything.

  • Full access to all pages & all trips
  • Can add team members and assign tasks
  • Handy when you're not around
Supervisor

Handles routes on the ground. Each supervisor manages their own trips and drivers and updates status as the trip moves.

  • Creates & runs only their own trips and tours
  • Updates live status, sends WhatsApp to drivers
  • Raises money requests to the accountant
  • Cannot see other supervisors' routes
Accountant

Runs the money side. Billing, expenses, payroll and reports — and approves the requests your supervisors raise.

  • Invoices & GST, expenses, payroll, reports
  • Approves / rejects advance & expense requests
  • Exports data to Excel

03Owner & Manager

Building your team & creating logins

OwnerManager

You can create as many users as you need. Each one gets their own email login and only the pages you allow.

  1. Go to Team & Roles in the sidebar and press + Add member.
  2. Enter their name, phone and email, and pick a role (Manager / Supervisor / Accountant).
  3. For supervisors & accountants, tick exactly which pages they can open. Owners & managers automatically get everything.
  4. Press Create account. The app shows a one-time temporary password — copy it and send it to that person over WhatsApp or in person.
  5. They open the website, sign in with that email + temp password, and set their own password. Done — they're on the team.

On the Team page each person shows their photo, role, phone, the pages they can access, and their live status for today. Tap Edit access to change a role or page permissions later, Assign task to give them a job, or Today to see what they've done.

Profiles & photos. Everyone can open My profile (top-right menu) to add their photo, phone and change their password. Their photo then shows across the app so you always know who's who.
04The home screen

Your dashboard (Overview)

The Overview changes depending on who's logged in.

Owner & Manager — the “Team mix”

At the top you see every teammate: whether they're online, on break or offline, how many tasks they have, and any overdue task in red. Press + Assign task to hand someone a job with a deadline. Below that are your live business numbers — revenue, profit, outstanding, active trips, trip status, fleet, document alerts.

Alerts with sound. When a teammate finishes a task or a deadline passes, you get a pop-up notification and a chime. Tip: the sound only starts after you've clicked once on the page (a browser rule) — after that it just works. The 🔔 bell top-right keeps your history; the speaker icon there toggles the sound.

Supervisor & Accountant — “Your day”

Workers see their own strip: the tasks assigned to them each with a timer (Start / Pause / Done), a Take a break button, and a Work log where they type what they did as they go (“Collected POD from Bharat Steels”). Simply having the app open counts as being at work — the owner sees your active hours automatically.

05The core of the business

Trips & Routes

Owner sees allManager sees allSupervisor: own routes

Every consignment is a trip. Each trip gets an automatic VR ID and LR number, a route, a driver & vehicle, and a live status you update as it moves.

Creating a trip

  1. Open Trips & Routes and press + New trip.
  2. Choose Single point (pickup → drop) or Multiple points to add as many stops as you like (pickup, in-between points, final drop).
  3. Type each location. Start typing and saved points suggest themselves (type “JP” → “JP Nagar”); new places are remembered for next time. Paste a Google Maps link under each point so the driver can tap to navigate.
  4. Pick the driver and vehicle. Not added yet? Press ＋ New right there to add a driver or customer without leaving the form.
  5. Choose the customer (optional), fill material, weight and freight. As owner/manager you can also set Handled by — which supervisor owns this route.
  6. Press Create trip. The VR ID & LR are stamped automatically.

Updating an ongoing trip — one tap

Each trip is a card. The green button always shows the next step — press it to advance: Heading to pickup → At pickup → each point → At drop → Finish. On Finish you add a short remark (“delivered fine”, “police checkpost delay”, “short payment”). That's the whole status update — one tap per stage.

Live tracking timeline

Press Track on any trip to open the tracking view: a moving 🚚 along a progress bar and a step-by-step timeline. Each stop shows its Open in Google Maps link. The person in charge marks each stage as the driver reaches it, so anyone looking knows exactly where the trip is.

Four supervisors, four routes. Give the Trips page to several supervisors and each handles their own routes — they can't see each other's trips. You (owner/manager) see everyone's, with a “handled by” label on every trip showing which supervisor is running it.
06Talk to drivers & customers

WhatsApp messages

Anyone with the page

The app writes your WhatsApp messages for you — you just pick the trip and send.

  1. Open WhatsApp and pick the trip from the dropdown (or search by VR / LR / route / driver).
  2. Three ready messages appear at once: Trip → driver (with the Google Maps links for each stop), Dispatch note → customer, and Payment reminder → customer.
  3. Press Open in WhatsApp to launch WhatsApp with the number and message already filled — just hit send. Or press Copy to paste it anywhere.
Why it helps: the driver gets the full route with tappable map links, the customer gets a clean dispatch note, and payment reminders go out in seconds — all in your company's voice, no typing.
07The money side

Expenses, invoices & payroll

AccountantOwnerManager

Expenses & Fuel

Press + Add expense. Mark whether it's for a trip, the office, or general. Pick a category, or choose “Other” to type a new category — it's saved for next time. Log diesel under Fuel log and the app auto-flags fuel leakage (diesel bought vs. distance ÷ mileage).

Raising a request to the accountant

Anyone can press Raise request for an advance or expense — a diesel advance for a driver, a puncture repair, etc. It appears in the Requests to accountant list, where the accountant presses Approve or Reject. The pending count shows on the Expenses page so nothing is missed.

Invoices

Under Invoices, raise a GST invoice for a customer (base + GST calculated for you), then mark it Paid when the money comes in. Outstanding invoices show on the dashboard so you always know who owes you.

Payroll — explained simply

Payroll is paying your drivers & staff. For each person: Base salary + Bhatta (per-trip allowance) − Deductions (advances, fines) = Net pay. Press Run payroll to mark the cycle paid — it's safe to press twice, it never double-pays. Past cycles show under “Recent payouts”.

08Everyone shares these

Customers, truck owners & fleet

Shared by the whole team

Customers

Your clients and their rate contracts. Add a customer here, or on the fly while creating a trip. Create and print an agreement for any customer in one step.

Truck Owners (market vehicles)

When you use other transporters' trucks, add the owner under Truck Owners, track what you owe them, record payments, and generate their attachment agreement or trial LOI.

Trucks & Drivers

Your fleet and drivers. Store each driver's Aadhaar & licence and each truck's RC, insurance & fitness — including photos of the documents. The dashboard warns you before any document expires.

Document photos. Upload a photo of any document; tap it to preview it full-screen first, with a Download button — it no longer just downloads blindly. Great for showing an RC or licence at a checkpost.
09See it & take it with you

Reports & data export

AccountantOwnerManager

Reports show revenue, profit, and your best vehicles & routes. Data Export turns any list into an Excel sheet with one click.

  1. Open Data Export.
  2. Press Download next to any dataset — Trips, Invoices, Expenses, Fuel, Customers, Payables, Payroll.
  3. The Excel opens in your Downloads. The Trips sheet includes the VR ID and “Handled by” column, so you can see which supervisor ran each route.

Use exports for your CA, GST filing, or to share numbers with a partner.

10Run the team

Tasks, timers & team activity

OwnerManager

Hand out jobs, set deadlines, and see what everyone is doing — without calling anyone.

  • Assign a task from the dashboard or a person's card. Add a finish-by time. It lands at the top of their Overview with a stopwatch.
  • They work it: Start (timer runs) → Pause → Done. If the deadline passes, it turns red for them and for you, and you get an alert + chime.
  • Work log: as they finish jobs they jot what they did. You see it under their Today.
  • Activity: opening the app = being at work. Their active hours (from–to) and “on break” status show on the Team page. The owner is not tracked — only the team is.
11Paperwork

Documents & printing

Every document opens as a clean preview first, with a button to print or save as PDF — it never downloads blindly.

  • Lorry Receipt (LR) — from any trip, press LR.
  • Tax invoice — from the Invoices page.
  • Agreements & LOI — from Customers and Truck Owners.
  • Document photos — tap to preview full-screen with a download option.

On the preview, press Print / Save as PDF and choose “Save as PDF” in your browser's print dialog to keep a copy or send it on WhatsApp/email.

13Good to know

Tips & FAQ

  • It works on the phone. Open the site, then “Add to Home screen” for an app-like icon.
  • Everything is live & shared. A trip a supervisor creates shows on your screen instantly — you don't pass files around.
  • Notification sound not playing? Click anywhere on the page once (a browser rule); after that the chime works. Toggle it with the speaker icon in the 🔔 bell.
  • Change your password any time in My profile (top-right).
  • Create as many users as you want — there's no limit. Give each only the pages they need.
  • Wrong person sees a page? Owner/Manager → Team & Roles → Edit access → untick the page.
